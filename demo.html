How FavicChain Works

Follow the step-by-step process to understand how our blockchain solution transforms land registry.

1

Title Submission

Property owners submit their land titles for verification through our secure portal or partner offices.

2

Document Verification

Our system cross-references submitted documents with government records and checks for inconsistencies or fraud indicators.

3

Blockchain Registration

Verified titles are hashed and recorded on the blockchain, creating an immutable, timestamped record of ownership.

4

Smart Contract Creation

For each property, a smart contract is deployed that manages ownership transfers and access permissions.

5

Access & Verification

Authorized parties can verify property ownership instantly through our platform, mobile app, or USSD service.
